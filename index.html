About Us

Founded in 1962, Stark Industries has established itself as a leader in high-pressure diesel and electric compressor packages. Based in Houston, Texas, our 40,000 sq. ft. facility is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to design and manufacture compressors that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our philosophy is simple: build dependable, easy-to-maintain machines that stand the test of time. This commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of major corporations and organizations worldwide.
