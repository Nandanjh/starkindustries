Our History

Stark Industries was founded in 1962 by Howard Stark, a visionary engineer who saw the need for reliable, high-performance compressors in the growing industrial sector. What began as a small workshop in Houston has grown into a global leader in compressor technology.

Throughout our 60+ year history, we've remained committed to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our compressors have been used in some of the most challenging environments on Earth, from deep-sea exploration to desert oil fields.

1962

Stark Industries founded in Houston, Texas

1975

Introduction of the first V-5 compressor series

1988

Expansion to international markets

2002

Opening of 40,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility

2015

Launch of eco-friendly compressor line

Today

Continuing our legacy of engineering excellence
